Letter dated 29 September 2014 from the Secretary-General addressed to the President of the Security Council

UN. Secretary-General

http://hdl.handle.net/11176/89330

Refers to the letter dated 16 July 2014 from the President of the Security Council (S/2014/504); transmits documents entitled "Assessment mission concerning the Liberia sanctions regime", requested by the Council members for an assessment mission by 1 Oct. 2014 on the progress that Liberia has made towards meeting the conditions set out in Council resolution 1521 (2003) for the termination of sanctions, and to provide recommendations on UN and other technical assistance to the Government of Liberia.
